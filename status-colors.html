Application Status Colors

CollabCareers candidate portal · portalStatusColor

Design handoff · 12 statuses

A distinct font + background for every portal status.

These are the statuses a candidate sees on their own applications — the right-hand column of your mapping. Each one now gets its own hue, so states no longer collide. Values map 1:1 to Tailwind's default palette, and every text-on-background pair clears WCAG AA (4.5:1) in both light and dark.

✦

What this fixes. In the current portal, Draft, Interview In Progress and Offer On Hold all render amber, and the three closed states share one gray — so different statuses look identical. This palette gives each a separate hue (or, for the neutral states, a separate shade) while keeping the brand pink #ED184F reserved for buttons and links, never for status.
